The Nine Charismatic Gifts

A prayer starter, not a verdict  ·  1 Corinthians 12:8–10

✋ Read this first

This is a conversation starter for prayer — not a test, and not discernment. No quiz can tell you your gifts.

Two questions matter more than any score here:

  • What has God already given me?
  • What is God desiring to give me?

There is no substitute for prayer. Real gifts are confirmed in the Church, tested by their fruit, and grown in deeper prayer and discernment — never self-declared. Bring your results to God, and to those who guide your formation.

1 · Where is your life right now?

Tap the areas where you're carrying the most. This shapes what the Spirit may be inviting — it doesn't score you.

Two columns, one prayer

Ordered gently, high to low. These are doors to explore, not labels to wear.

Already given?

Where you may already be recognizing the Spirit at work in your experience.

    Inviting you?

    Where your mission and desire suggest God may be drawing you to grow.

      Now the real work. Take the top gift in each column into 10 minutes of silent prayer this week. Ask the Lord plainly: “Is this from You? What are You asking of me?” Notice the fruit — does it build up love, or self? Bring it to a mentor, spiritual director, or your service team before acting on it.
